Our Story

Honest food from our family farm to yours

Ethno Foods (S V Agro Foods) is a Telangana-based natural foods maker on a simple belief — "ఆరోగ్యం ఎవరికీ ఊరికే రాదు, మీ ఆరోగ్యం మీ చేతుల్లోనే". Real health comes from real food.

We farm-source our grains and seeds, hand-clean them, and process them the traditional way — wood-pressed oils, stone-ground masalas, and herbal powders from our own farm. No colours, no preservatives, no shortcuts. FSSAI-licensed and delivered across India.
